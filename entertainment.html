Blockbuster Bengali Film “Shonar Shahar” Breaks All Records

By: P.Dey · Date: 12 August 25· Place:Kolkata

Anirban Chatterjee’s Shonar Shahar has become the highest-grossing Bengali film, collecting ₹120 crore in two weeks. Starring Rituparna Sengupta and Dev, the film’s music by Anupam Roy and Kolkata-set narrative drew packed theatres and critical acclaim.
